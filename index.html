Welcome to the Redwoods design system community.

We’re a community of life-long learners committed to supporting each other on the journey of design system maturity.

A space to hold each other up and support each other, just as Redwoods do.

Learn from the best in Design Systems.

Designers, Engineers, Program and Product Managers, we’re all here to learn from one another and grow together.

Group problem solving

We collaborate to collectively explore and solve design system issues as a team.

Career Support

We share our individual experiences across different disciplines and teams, fostering a culture of mutual learning.

Success Stories

We learn from each other’s experiences. Sharing what’s worked or not worked and why, as well as how we got there.

Mini-Master Classes

We share how-to’s on anything and everything design systems by and for the community.

Presentation Coaching

We coach each other on storytelling, slide design, and give feedback on individual presentations to level each other up.

Social Catchups

We want to get to know each other behind what we do for work. This is a safe space we can be ourselves.

Our values

Stay in learning mode.

Everyone, from juniors to seniors, has something to teach you.

Our community encourages continuous and open communication, promoting sharing by all.

We’re in this together.

We’re an extension of your team. Here to support, advise and guide you throughout your systems career.

Our purpose is to lift one another up.

Come as you are.

Respect is our default value. We are a safe and open community for you to come as you are and be your authentic self from personal through to professional.

This is a judgement free zone.
